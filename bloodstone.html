Activate Interact (manipulate) — Empower Spell

Frequency once per day
Requirements You are a prepared or spontaneous spellcaster.
Effect When you cast a spell of 1st level or higher, you can activate the Blood Stone to increase that spell’s level by 1 (to a maximum of 10th level). This functions like heightening a spell, improving its effects accordingly.

Curse — Life-Draining Stone
Whenever you activate the Blood Stone, you must attempt a DC 25 Fortitude save.

  • Critical Success No ill effect.
  • Success You lose 1 Hit Point permanently (maximum Hit Points are reduced).
  • Failure You lose a number of permanent Hit Points equal to your level.
  • Critical Failure You lose twice your level in permanent Hit Points.

These lost Hit Points reduce your maximum Hit Points until restored. Only a year of uninterrupted rest or powerful restorative magic (such as wish, miracle, or regenerate of a level equal to or higher than the heightened spell you cast) can restore them. You are unaware of this reduction unless someone succeeds at a DC 28 Occultism or Medicine check to identify the malady.

Demonic Awakening
The Blood Stone tracks the total amount of life-force it consumes. Once it has drained 100 Hit Points, it cracks open, releasing a blood demon (use an appropriate demon stat block, such as a kalavakus or custom demon) that is hostile to all creatures. After summoning the demon, the Blood Stone crumbles into worthless dust.

Craft Requirements You must be a spellcaster and have access to enervation and summon fiend.
